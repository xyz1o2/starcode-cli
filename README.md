**Starcode CLI**

A blazing-fast, lightweight AI-powered coding assistant built in Rust.

Starcode CLI brings powerful code intelligence directly to your terminal — whether you're writing new features, refactoring, debugging, or exploring unfamiliar codebases. It combines the speed and reliability of a native Rust application with intelligent assistance that feels natural and responsive.

### Why Starcode CLI?

- **Lightning fast startup** — Launches instantly, no waiting around.
- **Extremely lightweight** — Minimal memory footprint, perfect for running alongside your editor and other tools.
- **Zero dependencies** — Single static binary. No Python, Node.js, or heavy runtimes required.
- **Simple installation** — Just download the binary for your platform and go.

Whether you prefer piping code, chatting in the terminal, or integrating it into your workflow with scripts, Starcode CLI is designed to stay out of your way until you need it.

Built for developers who value performance, simplicity, and reliability.
